About

The PL Reading Group (PLRG) is the de facto successor to PL Wonks and several previous reading groups. Unlike the PL Wonks talk series, the focus of PLRG is paper reading and discussion. However, we do occasionally schedule presentations, practice talks, and tutorial sessions (Schedule).

We are looking for presentations! Please refer to Participation for details about voting for papers and scheduling your presentation.

Time

  • <10 AM> on Thursdays.
  • The event usually lasts 1 hour, but you may leave early.
  • If you are a speaker, please make sure your presentation is within 40 minutes.

Location

  • BLIF 3069 (conference room, 3rd floor Luddy Hall) (Campus Map)
